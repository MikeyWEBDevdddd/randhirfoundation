About Us!

Randhir Foundation is an Educational Startup (EdTech), focusing on Affordability of Courses in online mode. We want to provide EDUCATION / SKILLS / TECHNOLOGY without any difference in caste/religion or rich/poor family in Manipur.

Our Vision!

To be a leading online education platform of Manipur.

Why Randhir Foundation?

Our goal is to provide quality education to everyone in very affordable fees, we believe equality in education will improve every students, thus resulting in betterment in society whether they belong to Rich family or Middle class family or Poor family.
